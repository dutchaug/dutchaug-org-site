Support Us!

Feb 28th, 2013

Financial support

The Dutch Android User Group is an official foundation and therefore is not seeking to gain any profit in organizing meetups. It is no secret that organizing meetups costs money. A normal meetup starts around 4:30pm and ends around 9:00pm. We will always serve dinner, drinks, snacks, coffee and tea during these meetups. If you want to support our initiative please get in touch and we will send you a document (currently in Dutch) with some background information on The Dutch Android User Group and the sponsoring possibilities.

Location sponsor

If you have a location which would seat around 30 to 100 people and are willing to let us utilize this location for one of our meetups please let us know as well.

Presenting a talk or give a workshop yourself!

If you are a leader in a specific Android topic and have some insights to share send us the title and a short description of your presentation or workshop (500 words) plus a short biography (500 words), previous speaker experience and a picture to info@dutchaug.org. We will get back to you shortly if your talk fits one of our upcoming meetup topics.
