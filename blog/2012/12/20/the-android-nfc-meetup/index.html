The Android NFC Meetup

Dec 20th, 2012

Join the conversation

On Thursday December 20th we will pick-up our meetup schedule for the 2nd year of The Dutch Android User Group. At droidconNL we had lots of fun with “The NFC Hunt” which inspired us to do more with NFC in the future. Luckily we have a pretty great line-up of speakers who have had many years of experience with NFC, recently available to a wide spread audience of people using the latest Android devices. We only have 80 seats available so join sooner rather than later!

Theme: “The Android NFC Meetup!”

When you put two Android phones with NFC (Near Field Communication) capabilities close to each other… real close… in some apps a “Touch to Beam” dialog pops out and you can start sending pictures, or contact details, or whatever the programmers have come up with. NFC is an interesting way to communicate and which the latest Android devices are bringing to the masses. In this meetup we want to focus on what NFC is, what it’s future potential is and how you can use the “Touch to Beam” API in your own Android app and create whole new experiences for your end-users.

Featured speaker: Google’s NFC Tech Lead Martijn Coenen

We are very excited to announce that Google’s NFC Tech Lead Martijn Coenen will kick-off the NFC meetup with an interesting mix of inspirational and visionary talk about Android & NFC, but also very practical stuff on why and how you should introduce the “Touch To Beam” functionality in your app.

We hope to see you all on Dec 20th!
