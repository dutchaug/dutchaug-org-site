You Can Hack, but You Can't Hide!

Nov 24th, 2013

Sunday November 24th 2013 | 9am - 9pm | Science Park Amsterdam

The Dutch Android User Group and droidconNL invite you to join the coolest Android hackathon event of the year!

Location, location, location

Are you a savvy Android developer or designer but have little time to play around with all the cool new Google Play APIs and Android features? Or are you lacking inspiration or quality time to think up the next generation Android apps? If you can’t hide your enthusiasm for Android and just enjoy building great Android apps this event is for you! Sign up and be inspired by your teammates and fellow contestants and measure your Android skills in a single day Android hackathon event, just before the biggest Android conference in The Netherlands, droidconNL!

Location, location, location:

The hackathon will be held on Sunday November 24th at the droidconNL 2013 venue; Science Park Amsterdam. We open the doors at 9am and explain the process and rules and start the hackathon a little after that. Then you have until 8pm to finish up your Android app and present it to a jury of Android experts. Apart from great prizes you’ll win eternal glory for winning the second DutchAUG hackathon and you can brag about your winning Android app for a whole year! At the end of the hackathon all contestant are invited to the ”after-the-hack party” for an informal meet & greet with droidconNL speakers, sponsors and VIPs.

How can I join?

You can sign-up at http://www.droidcon.nl starting from Monday July 29th at 10am! We will allow 12 teams to register for this event so be sure to register sooner rather than later if you want to enter the competition. The entry fee for this competition is €25,- per person, which is used to cover some of the costs for lunch, dinner, drinks and snacks throughout the day. You can either sign-up as a solo Android developer or designer or sign-up with a complete team. The ideal team consist of two Android developers and one designer (the top teams from last year had two developers and a designer on board), but teams of three Android developers are allowed as well. We encourage Android development companies to register and sponsor their own team!

Further information

If you need further information about the hackathon or want to contact the organization about registering a company team or other sponsoring possibilities please send an e-mail to info@dutchaug.org.
