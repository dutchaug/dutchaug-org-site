DutchAUG Becomes a Foundation

Feb 5th, 2013

Today, we have registered +The Dutch Android User Group as an official foundation at the notary office this morning and at the chamber of commerce later this week.

Formalising our initiative helps us in creating better content for you, the Dutch Android professional or hobbyist. By becoming a foundation we are better equiped to attract funds and are committed to give everything back to the Dutch Android community in the form of meetups, open space meetings, hackathons, workshops, etc. We are excited, confident and looking forward to continue to build the Dutch Android community with Stichting (Foundation in Dutch) The Dutch Android User Group in the upcoming years!

In the pictures, foundation board members +Johan Pelgrim, +Dennis Geurts and +Wiebe Elsinga jotting down their signatures on the notary papers.
